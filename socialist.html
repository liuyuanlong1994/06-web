伟大领袖毛主席万岁!

Long live the great leader Chairman Mao!

Mao Zedong, one of the founders of People's Republic of China, a national idol for generations of people both domestically and internationally, died in 1976. Mao is not only a revolutionist, but also a poet. During his life, he wrote thousands of poetry in classical Chinese form. He is also a public intellectual, whose polemic against orthodox Lenism originated his own "Maoism".

On the other hand, Mao is also blamed for initiating the "Cultural Revolution" and "A Great Leap Forward", where communist bureaucrats were incentivized to report fake production figures, in order to compete with the West. As a result, millions of Chinese died of fame in these political movements, aside from those died for political struggle. Nevertheless, Mao's influence still reached other countries such as Japan, France and the U.S..
